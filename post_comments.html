You can use [LRCGET](https://github.com/tranxuanthang/lrcget). You just select the folder where your music is and it scans and tries to download lyrics for all tracks available in its database. <br><br>For listening music on my computer and organizing it I love MusicBee.<br><br>And for listening on my phone I use Musicolet however if you like Poweramp just keep using it, it's already one of the best apps for the purpose.<br><br>Edit: I just realized that you want to embed the synced lyrics to the files. I don't think that's possible, however someone correct me if I'm wrong. The software I linked downloads a .LRC file with the same name as the music file and then your music app of choice reads it and displays the synced lyrics.
